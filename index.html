Augmented Reality Part builder

One of the major projects I worked on in Iknowledge was an application for the Microsoft Hololens. It was meant to be a proof of concept that Augmented reality could be used and be beneficial to Electronic Work Instruction(EWI). EWI are software designed for manufacturing, it presents the instructions on how a worker is to accomplish their assigned tasks alongside any tools that are required and any tests or inspections needed to be carried out on a screen. The Part Builder App was to take this and apply it to an Augmented Reality environment.

The Slow Escape

This was a project my cousin and I started together in Autumn 2019. My cousin wanted to learn a bit about coding and make a game so we collaborated over 3 months in our spare time together to create "The Slow Escape".
The Slow Escape is a platformer inspired by the games Give Up games and Super Meat Boy. Platforming occurs on a level by level basis where you must complete the previous level to unlock the next. Individual Level structure split out out into several stages, each stage has a goal that the player must reach to advance to the next stage. Level stages overlap on top of another so the challenge increases the further into the level you progress.
Some Features of the game:

  • 12 levels
  • Progressive damage that shows on the character(per level)
  • 125 achievements
  • Save files
Once we got the game to a very unpolished but completed state we decided to end development for various reasons.
